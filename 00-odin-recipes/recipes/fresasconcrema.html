Strawberry Dessert

Description

Fresas con crema is a classic Mexican dessert made with fresh strawberries and sweet cream, often blended with condensed milk and vanilla. It's a simple, refreshing treat loved for its creamy, fruity flavor.

A total prep and cook time of 15 minutes, sure to be a perfect dessert for the summertime!

Ingredients

  • 2lbs strawberries
  • 1 can of media crema
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup of sweetended condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup of sour cream

Steps

  1. Dice the strawberries to preference and add to a medium size bowl
  2. Combine the media crema and 3/4 cup of diced strawberries in a blender or food processor, blend until smooth
  3. Pour the cream over the strawberries, stir to combine
  4. let it chill in the fridge, and is ready to serve when chilled
